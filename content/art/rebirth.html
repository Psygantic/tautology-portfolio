Rebirth is an LED lightboard built by Danne Stayskal and John Windberg displaying a modified version of an artificial life algorithm. It will be present in the light garden surrounding the inflection art project at Burning Man 2015.

More on rebirth is available on Danne's portfolio. Full Arduino source for this build is available on GitHub.
